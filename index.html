§ 02 · Approach

Three promises. Held at the sensor.

P.01

Trust-first, from the device up.

Every observation is anchored to a specific device, place, and moment, and signed before it leaves the field. What you receive is not a data point. It is testimony.

P.02

Built to wait, by design.

The field node is built around duty-cycled wake, solar + LiFePO4 power, and a secure element that never streams raw data: a deliberate hardware design, now moving from architecture to build.

§ 03 · Method

Four movements.
One sealed record.

TerraProof does not stream raw data. It watches, interprets, seals, and hands you a record that travels without losing its integrity.

01 · Detect

Watch for what matters.

An on-device detection model scans every frame and localizes every animal in shot. Detection is class-agnostic, so nothing is missed for want of a matching category. Validated against real camera-trap imagery at pilot scale, engineered to run behind a duty-cycled wake trigger in the field.

02 · Interpret

On-device intelligence.

When an event occurs, TerraProof wakes local compute to classify it: an on-device classification model scoring against a taxonomy of around 2,000 species. Species, individual count, confidence, resolved per frame, no cloud round-trip required.

03 · Sign

Tamper-evident, sealed in place.

The event is bound to its device, coordinates, and timestamp, hashed, and signed with the private key before it ever touches a network. Alter one field afterward and the signature breaks: visibly, specifically, provably.

04 · Export

Evidence that travels without losing weight.

Every record links to the previous one in a hash chain, then feeds into ESRS E4-5, TNFD Core, and Ökokonto / §41 BNatSchG-aligned reports, with full chain of custody from field to filing. Anyone downstream can verify, cold.

§ 05 · The architecture

Verification at the source.

A system designed to generate defensible ecosystem intelligence by combining local cryptographics, edge inference, and satellite baseline validation.

Sec.01

Cryptographic Ledger

Every observation is bound to local metadata and signed using ECDSA (P-256) via the secure hardware element. Detections are grouped and hash-chained sequentially, ensuring that any downstream modification breaks the integrity check.

  • ECDSA P-256 + SHA-256 signature
  • Hardware secure key storage
  • Sequential ledger hash-chaining
Sec.02

Ecosystem Condition Index

A composite index combining fauna indicators (richness, abundance, and conservation-status weightings) with acoustic complexity metrics and Sentinel-2 NDVI satellite structure data to synthesize a baseline-calibrated score.

  • Visual & Acoustic sub-indices
  • NDVI canopy structure fusion
  • Site-calibrated baseline scoring
Sec.03

Edge Inference Engine

Run locally on an optimized edge stack: class-agnostic animal detection paired with an on-device species classifier. Acoustic sensors perform duty-cycled bird/bat call sweeps, engineered for ultra-low standby power draw.

  • Local ONNX / ExecuTorch inference
  • Duty-cycled acoustic audio sweeps
  • Ultra-low-power runtime specs

§ 09 · Field notes

Questions we get,
answered plainly.

Q.01What is TerraProof, in one sentence?+
A working edge-AI pipeline that detects and classifies wildlife, cryptographically signs every observation, and scores ecosystem condition, with a physical field node as the next step.
Q.02Is this just a smarter camera trap?+
No. Camera traps record. TerraProof interprets, signs, and vouches. The output is a structured, tamper-evident event, not a media file you have to defend after the fact.
Q.03Who is it for?+
Conservation projects, restoration operators, biodiversity monitoring teams, researchers, and corporate sustainability teams who need their field evidence to survive scrutiny from regulators, auditors, or registries.
Q.04Is the hardware available yet?+
The detection and scoring software is live today: request a walkthrough of the full dashboard now. The field hardware node is in active engineering development: solar-powered, duty-cycled, built for multi-year autonomous deployment. We're bringing on early pilot partners now to help shape it before the first units ship.
Q.05Does this replace eDNA, acoustics, or satellite?+
No, and in fact we fuse with one of them directly: real Sentinel-2 satellite NDVI feeds our Structure sub-index alongside the fauna signal. Think of TerraProof as the trust layer and fusion engine across these methods, not a replacement for any one. (NDVI is a greenness proxy, not biodiversity itself; we treat it as one signal, never the whole picture.)
Q.06Is the Ecosystem Condition Index certified or independently validated?+
Every score ships with a confidence tier and its full component breakdown, never a bare number. Reference conditions are calibrated per site and refined through ongoing ecological review as we scale. Cryptographic signing proves the data is unaltered and traceable to its device; it doesn't by itself certify the ecology; we keep those two claims separate, by design, on every record.
